Material toasts (snackbars)

Toasts, known in Angular as 'snackbars', provide brief messages about app processes at the bottom of the screen.

Usage

Toasts inform users of a process that an app has performed or will perform. They appear temporarily, towards the bottom of the screen. They shouldn't interrupt the user experience, and they don't require user input to disappear.

Principles

Informational - Toasts provide updates on an app's processes.

Temporary - Toasts appear temporarily, and disappear on their own, without requiring user input.

Contextual - Toasts are placed in the most suitable area of the UI.

Guidance

Toasts triggered by buttons

Toasts with no action should dismiss automatically.

Toasts with an action should wait for the user.
